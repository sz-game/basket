How to Play Basket Random on All Devices

Basket Random is incredibly easy to learn and available on all devices - just grab a friend and start bouncing! The simple one-button controls make it perfect for quick matches, whether you're on desktop, mobile, or tablet. Follow the device-specific controls below to master this hilarious basketball experience.


🖥️ Desktop Controls

Action Control
Player 1 Jump/Shoot ⌨️ W Key
Player 2 Jump/Shoot ⌨️ Up Arrow Key
Start Game 🖱️ Click Play Button
Menu Navigation 🖱️ Mouse Click

🎯 Desktop Tip: Hold the key longer to control your player's jump height and direction!

📱 Mobile Controls

Action Control

Player 2 Jump/Shoot 👆 Tap Right Side Button

Menu Navigation 👆 Touch Screen
